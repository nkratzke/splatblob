How to enter the Hall of Fame?

  1. Study Computer Science at the Lübeck University of Applied Sciences.
  2. Pass the webtechnology course with a surpassing grade.
  3. Allow that your webtechnology project outcome can be made available for public.
  4. The five best game outcomes will be posted to the Google+ Dartisans community.
  5. The Google+ Dartisans community votes whether your game is the best.
  6. The three most voted games will enter the "Hall of Fame".
  7. Only the #1 game will get a grading bonus.

Who is responsible?

Nane Kratzke, Professor for Computer Science at Lübeck University of Applied Sciences
