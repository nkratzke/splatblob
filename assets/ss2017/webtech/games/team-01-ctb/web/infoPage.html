How To Play

Description:

Catch the BallZ with a different color from your basket.
Letting a BallZ wth a different color through or catching a BallZ with the same color as your Basket, will decrease your lifes count by one.

The basket will change its color randomly.
Catching a BallZ with a modifier will activate its effect.


Controls:

Touch the left or right half of your touchscreen to move the basket one lane to the corresponding side.


Modifier:

Slows down the dropping speed of the BallZ
Speeds up the dropping speed of the BallZ
Increases your lives count by one
Enables the collecting of all BallZ
Removes all BallZ from the field instantly
Prevents taking damage by the wrong color of BallZ
Changes the color of the basket randomly
Multiplicates the points for catching BallZ by 2
Multiplicates the points for catching BallZ by 3
Adapts to and catches BallZ automatically. Cancel by moving
