Controls:

Left Arrow Key - move left
Right Arrow Key - move right

Rules:

Your goal is to destroy every brick in a level to proceed to the next one.
The ball will bounce off of the player in three different directions (top-left / up / top-right)
and will destroy the brick by hitting it enough times (green = 1 / yellow = 2 / red = 3 hits).
The player has lost the game if the ball touches the bottom side of the screen.
