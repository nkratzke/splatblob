How To Play

You are a furious and fearless warrior living in a cruel world populated by dangerous monsters. Dive into the adventure and try to save the world. As the chosen one you need to kill all the seven dungeon bosses to achieve your aim.

- move your warrior by clicking where you want to go
- the fighting system is turn-based and each skill is different
- enemies differ in strength and stamina
- the enemies get stronger each stage you complete
- items can be obtained by both treasures and monsters
- new found items are moved directly into your inventory
- equip items to get stronger, deal more and resist damage
